Vera5 Cookie Policy

Effective Date: June 29, 2026 Last Updated: June 29, 2026

Vera5 does not intentionally use cookies on its website.

The Vera5 website at https://vera5.io is a static informational website. It does not currently use cookies for:

  • advertising;
  • behavioral tracking;
  • analytics;
  • user accounts;
  • authentication;
  • preferences;
  • personalization;
  • payment processing; or
  • session management.

Vera5 also does not intentionally use:

  • tracking pixels;
  • advertising identifiers;
  • browser fingerprinting;
  • session-replay tools;
  • cross-site tracking technologies; or
  • similar technologies used to monitor visitors across unrelated websites.

Because the Vera5 website does not intentionally use nonessential cookies, Vera5 does not currently display a cookie-consent banner.

Website Hosting and Technical Records

Our website hosting and infrastructure providers may process limited technical information necessary to deliver, secure, and maintain the website.

This may include:

  • IP address;
  • request date and time;
  • requested page or file;
  • browser type;
  • operating system;
  • response status;
  • network information;
  • approximate geographic information; and
  • security or abuse-detection signals.

This technical information may be processed through standard server logs or infrastructure systems. It is not intentionally used by Vera5 for advertising, behavioral profiling, or cross-site tracking.

External Websites

The Vera5 website may contain links to third-party websites, including:

  • GitHub;
  • the Chrome Web Store;
  • threat-intelligence providers;
  • documentation services;
  • social-media platforms; and
  • other external resources.

Those external websites may use their own cookies or tracking technologies.

Vera5 does not control the cookie practices of third-party websites. Their use of cookies is governed by their own privacy policies and cookie policies.

Future Changes

If Vera5 later introduces functionality that uses cookies or similar technologies, such as:

  • user accounts;
  • authentication;

  • subscription features,
